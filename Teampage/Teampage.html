Our Team

Elon Mask

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur, engineer, and inventor. He is the CEO and lead designer of SpaceX, a private aerospace manufacturer and space transportation company. Musk is also the CEO of Tesla, a pioneering electric vehicle and clean energy company. His visionary work extends to renewable energy with SolarCity and ambitious plans for Mars colonization through SpaceX.

Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg is an American entrepreneur and technology innovator. He is the co-founder and CEO of Facebook (now Meta Platforms, Inc.). Zuckerberg played a pivotal role in creating and expanding the world's largest social media platform, connecting billions of people globally. He's also known for his efforts to explore the development of the metaverse, a virtual shared space, and the evolution of social networking.

Bill Gates

Bill Gates is an American software developer, businessman, and philanthropist. He is best known as the co-founder of Microsoft Corporation, a leading technology company that played a pivotal role in the personal computer revolution. Gates' leadership led to the widespread adoption of the Windows operating system. In his post-Microsoft career, he has dedicated himself to philanthropy through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, addressing global health, education, and poverty issues.

Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos is an American entrepreneur and business magnate. He is the founder of Amazon, one of the world's largest e-commerce and cloud computing companies, which he started in 1994. Under Bezos's leadership, Amazon transformed the way people shop online and expanded into various industries. He also owns The Washington Post and founded Blue Origin, a private space exploration company, with the goal of making space more accessible and promoting innovation in space technology.
