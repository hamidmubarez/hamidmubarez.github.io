The Second Amendment

Introduction

Society today operates under a framework of laws that have evolved with the development of civilized societies. Among these laws, the Second Amendment of the United States Constitution grants citizens the right to bear arms, a fundamental aspect of American society. While the Second Amendment has been effective in maintaining a sense of security, it is crucial to recognize that the issue lies not in the amendment itself but in the responsible ownership and regulation of firearms. This article argues that instead of attempting to alter or eliminate the Second Amendment, the focus should be on implementing comprehensive background checks for individuals seeking to own firearms
